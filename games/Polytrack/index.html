How to Play PolyTrack

Game Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys to drive
  • R or Enter to restart the track

Editor Controls

  • Left Mouse Button to build
  • Middle Mouse Button to rotate the view
  • Right Mouse Button to pan the view
  • Shift + Mouse Wheel to go up or down
  • R to rotate the selected part

Frequently Asked Questions

What is PolyTrack?

PolyTrack is a low-poly racing game heavily inspired by TrackMania, where you race against the clock to improve your time on different tracks. The game features loops, jumps, and high speeds where every millisecond counts.

Can I create my own tracks?

Yes! PolyTrack includes a level editor where you can create your own tracks. You can then export and share these tracks with other players.

What platforms can I play PolyTrack on?

PolyTrack is available on HTML5, Windows, macOS, and Linux platforms, so you can play it on virtually any device.

Is PolyTrack available in multiple languages?

Yes, PolyTrack supports 16 languages including Arabic, German, English, Spanish, French, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Polish, Portuguese, Russian, Turkish, Ukrainian, and Chinese.

Can I play PolyTrack on mobile devices?

Yes, PolyTrack supports touchscreen inputs, making it compatible with smartphones and tablets.

Is PolyTrack a multiplayer game?

PolyTrack is primarily a singleplayer game where you compete against the clock. However, you can share your tracks and times with friends to compete indirectly.

Where can I find more tracks to play?

You can find more tracks shared by the community on the official PolyTrack Discord server and Reddit page.
